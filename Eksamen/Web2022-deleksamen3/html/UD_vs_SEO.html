UD vs SEO

All websites chase the goal of success. But to actually become successful, the website needs to reach out to a bigger variety of people. There are two methods that can be implemented into your website that can help out with your goal; universal design (UD) and search engine optimization (SEO). This article will explore the two processes and will look into how they can be implemented and beneficial for your web development.

Universal Design

Universal design is not the first thing that pops up to a developer's head. But it can be a big issue later in time. UD is a process used to develop environments or products that can be accessed by people with special abilities, disabilities and various age groups. By taking these groups into consideration, the website becomes user friendly to the maximum number of users.

The best technique to use, when implementing universal design into web developing, is by following the 7 recommended principles:

Explanation

Example

1. Equitable use

2. Flexibility in use

3. Simple and intuitive use

4. Perceptible information

5. Tolerance for error

6. Low physical effort

7. Size and space for approach and use

Considering all the users, not just your target group. Think of possible issues people with diverse abilities, disabilities can encounter.
Providing choices, so the user can adjust their experience to their needs.
Making your design less complex, by removing unnecessary content and adjusting it to the person's preferences.
Effective exposure to content based on the users needs.
Adjusting the design to possible errors that the user can encounter accidentally.
Altering your design to the minimum physical effort.
Considering the device your website will be used on.
Text to speech software for blind people.
Zooming in or out, for people with vision issues.
Language settings. Article cards on the front page, to make it easier to locate the content you need.
Including captions in visual content.
Giving guidance or warnings when incorrect information is typed in.
Short cuts can be made with a keyboard.
Adjusting the contents layout and size on pc and phone.

Search Engine Optimization

Search engine optimization is different from UD, since it deals with the website's traffic, instead of its design. SEO is used to improve the ranking of a website on a search engine, such as Google, depending on its content relevance. With the help of algorithms, search engines can evaluate the popularity of the links, keywords and even the structure used in your code.

Since web development is becoming more accessible for more people , it can be difficult to compete with other websites on the world wide web. By using SEO it can increase your chances for people to see your web page, when searching up a relative topic. Here are a few tips that can help you rank higher on the search engine:
